IMPORTANT INFORMATION

COVID Guidelines and Vaccination Requirements

• Documents Required: All travellers must either carry their COVID vaccination certificate (2 doses taken) or a negative RT-PCR test report with sample taken within 72 hours before arrival.
• Test on Arrival: Travellers arriving in emergency situations such as a death in the family, medical treatment might have to take an on-arrival test and further action will be decided on the basis of test result.
• If you have arrived on any international flight and are taking a connecting domestic flight, Please check the 'Travelling to India Guidelines'.

Pre-Flight Checklist

• Remember to web check-in before arriving at the airport; carry a printed or soft copy of the boarding pass.
• Wearing masks/face covers is no longer mandatory. However, all travellers are advised to wear them, in view of the threat posed by COVID-19.
• One hand bag up to 7 kgs and 55x35x25cm, is allowed per traveller as cabin baggage. Certain personal articles like a lady's purse, laptop bags, etc. can be carried additionally.
